How to use the translation workflow

Follow these steps to improve video quality, make your gestures clearer, and get more consistent recognition results from the app.

videocam
Step 1

Open Camera

Start by clicking Open Camera in the translation page. Allow camera access when your browser asks for permission.

accessibility_new
Step 2

Position Yourself Clearly

Face the camera directly and keep your upper body and both hands visible. Better framing helps the system understand your sign motion more accurately.

info Keep your hands clearly visible and avoid cutting them off from the frame.
schedule
Step 3

Choose Recording Duration

Select the recording length based on your gesture. Short signs can use shorter duration, while longer gestures may need more time.

timer Recording length
4 seconds expand_more
radio_button_checked
Step 4

Record the Gesture

Press Record and perform your sign clearly. You can stop early if you finish before the selected duration ends.

analytics
Step 5

Analyze the Video

Click Analyze to process the recorded clip. Wait until the system completes the recognition before starting another recording.

fact_check
Step 6

Review the Recognition Result

The detected label appears on the preview area. Use this result as a guide and try recording again if the gesture was unclear or incomplete.

lightbulb

Pro Tip: Improve Lighting

Avoid strong backlight behind you. Front-facing light makes hand shape and motion easier to capture, which can improve recognition consistency.
